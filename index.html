Terms & Promissory Note

Principal & interest: The borrower borrows the stated principal; interest is fixed at 40% per month. Total amount payable equals principal + interest.

Repayment options: Full lump-sum on due date, or two-month instalment plan: Month 1: 80% of total; Month 2: remaining balance plus 40% additional on remainder (see example in the promissory note).

Late payments: Late payment incurs a penalty of 1% per day of the total amount (Principal + Interest) for up to three days. After 3 days the lender may take possession of agreed collateral. MyFinanceClub allows payment of the principal only on the due date if borrower chooses; the daily 1% rule continues to apply to interest.

Prepayment: Borrower may prepay at any time without penalty.

Other conditions: By submitting, borrower agrees to the promissory note and authorizes MyFinanceClub to verify bank details and, if necessary, contact provided references. The lender reserves rights specified in the promissory note.
